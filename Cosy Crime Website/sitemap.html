About Cosy Crime

Cosy Crime is the specialist mystery imprint of Rhubarb Press, dedicated exclusively to traditional cosy crime fiction. We focus on amateur sleuths, puzzle plots, and British-style village mysteries.

Submission Focus

We only consider manuscripts that clearly fit the cosy crime genre: minimal violence, close-knit settings, amateur detectives, and traditional fair-play mysteries. Please review our detailed guidelines before submitting.

About Cosy Crime

Cosy Crime is the dedicated mystery imprint of Rhubarb Press, specialising in the finest tradition of British crime fiction. We champion stories where wit trumps violence and clever detection solves the day.

From village mysteries to country house murders, our carefully curated collection features amateur sleuths, puzzle plots, and that distinctly British charm that makes cosy crime so enduringly popular. Each title promises fair-play detection and satisfying resolutions.

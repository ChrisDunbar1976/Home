Cosy Crime Submission Guidelines

Please note: We focus exclusively on cosy crime fiction. Before submitting, please ensure your manuscript fits within this traditional mystery genre.

What We Publish

  • Traditional cosy crime mysteries
  • Amateur detective protagonists
  • Village or small community settings
  • Fair-play puzzle plots
  • British or British-style settings

Submission Requirements

Please see our dedicated submissions page for detailed guidelines on manuscript formatting, required documents, and our submission process.

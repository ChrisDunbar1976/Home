Cosy Crime Submission Guidelines

Professional guidelines for submitting your cosy crime manuscript to our specialist imprint

Cosy Crime Specialists

Cosy Crime accepts submissions for traditional cosy crime fiction only. We are a specialist imprint of Rhubarb Press dedicated exclusively to this beloved mystery genre.

What We Publish

Cosy Crime Fiction

We specialise exclusively in traditional cosy crime fiction, featuring amateur sleuths, fair-play detection, and puzzle-driven mysteries set in close-knit communities. Our ideal manuscripts include:

  • Traditional British-style mysteries
  • Amateur detective protagonists
  • Village, small town, or community settings
  • Puzzle-focused plots with fair-play clues
  • Light humour and character-driven narratives
  • Violence occurs off-page

The Golden Age Tradition

We follow the classic tradition established by Agatha Christie, Dorothy L. Sayers, and their contemporaries:

  • Fair-play detection (all clues available to readers)
  • Closed-circle mysteries preferred
  • Satisfying logical solutions
  • Restoration of order and justice
  • Series potential welcomed

Submission Requirements

Manuscript Specifications

Format Requirements

  • File Format: Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx) or PDF
  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman or Arial
  • Line Spacing: Double-spaced throughout
  • Margins: 1-inch margins on all sides
  • Page Numbers: Centred in footer
  • Header: Author surname and abbreviated title on each page

Length Guidelines

  • Word Count: Minimum 70,000 words
  • Page Count: Approximately 280+ pages
  • Chapter Length: 2,000-4,000 words per chapter
  • Series Potential: Standalone or series welcome

Required Documents

  1. 1
    Query Letter (1 page maximum)

    Professional introduction with book summary and cosy crime credentials

  2. 2
    Synopsis (2-3 pages maximum)

    Complete plot summary including the solution

  3. 3
    Sample Chapters (First 3 chapters or 50 pages)

    Opening chapters demonstrating your mystery-writing style

  4. 4
    Author Biography (1 page maximum)

    Mystery writing experience and relevant background

Complete Manuscript Required

Your cosy crime novel must be complete before submission. We do not accept proposals for unfinished mysteries.

Exclusive Genre Focus

We only consider traditional cosy crime manuscripts. Please ensure your work fits this specific genre before submitting.

Fair-Play Detection

Your mystery must provide all necessary clues for readers to solve alongside your detective. No hidden evidence or last-minute revelations.

Query Letter Guidelines

Your query letter should demonstrate understanding of the cosy crime genre and highlight your manuscript's traditional mystery elements:

Opening Paragraph

  • • Book title and word count
  • • Genre: Cosy Crime Fiction
  • • Brief hook emphasising mystery elements
  • • Setting and amateur sleuth introduction

Middle Paragraph(s)

  • • The crime and initial investigation
  • • Your amateur detective's motivation
  • • Key suspects and red herrings
  • • Comparable cosy crime titles

Closing Paragraph

  • • Mystery writing credentials
  • • Knowledge of cosy crime genre
  • • Series potential if applicable
  • • Professional closing

Submission Process

How to Submit

1

Email all materials to: submissions@rhubarbpress.com

2

Subject line: "COSY CRIME SUBMISSION: [Your Book Title] by [Your Name]"

3

Attach all documents as separate files (do not embed in email body)

4

Include a brief cover email confirming your manuscript is traditional cosy crime

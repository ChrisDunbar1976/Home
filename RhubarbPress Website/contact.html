Submission Guidelines

Please note: Please review our detailed submission guidelines to ensure your manuscript meets our current requirements and formatting standards.

What to Include

  • Query letter (one page)
  • Synopsis (1-2 pages)
  • First three chapters
  • Author biography
  • Word count and genre

Response Time

We aim to respond to all submissions within 8-12 weeks. Due to the volume of submissions we receive, we can only respond to submissions that interest us.

Please do not submit your manuscript to multiple publishers simultaneously. We appreciate exclusive submissions during our review period.

Office Hours

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM GMT

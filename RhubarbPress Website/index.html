About Rhubarb Press

Founded with a passion for literature and a commitment to new voices, Rhubarb Press is an independent publishing house that believes in the enduring power of a good story. We champion authors who challenge, inspire, and entertain.

Our mission is to cultivate a diverse catalog of high-quality fiction and non-fiction, connecting readers with narratives that leave a lasting impression. We pride ourselves on a collaborative approach, working closely with our authors every step of the way from manuscript to market.

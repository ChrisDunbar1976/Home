Submission Guidelines

Professional guidelines for submitting your manuscript to Rhubarb Press

Currently Accepting Submissions

Rhubarb Press welcomes unagented submissions across all literary genres. Unlike major publishers such as HarperCollins, Penguin Random House, and Hodder & Stoughton, we accept direct submissions from authors without requiring literary agent representation.

What We Publish

Cosy Crime Fiction

While we have a particular fondness for cosy crime fiction, we welcome manuscripts across all genres. Our cosy crime titles feature amateur sleuths, minimal violence, and puzzle-driven mysteries set in close-knit communities. These manuscripts include:

  • Traditional British-style mysteries
  • Amateur detective protagonists
  • Village, small town, or community settings
  • Puzzle-focused plots with fair-play clues
  • Light humour and character-driven narratives

Rhubarb Press

Rhubarb Press actively welcomes diverse literary voices across multiple genres:

  • Hard-boiled and noir fiction
  • Psychological thrillers and horror
  • Non-fiction and memoirs
  • Children's and young adult fiction
  • Poetry and short story collections

Submission Requirements

Manuscript Specifications

Format Requirements

  • File Format: Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx) or PDF
  • Font: 12-point Times New Roman or Arial
  • Line Spacing: Double-spaced throughout
  • Margins: 1-inch margins on all sides
  • Page Numbers: Centred in footer
  • Header: Author surname and abbreviated title on each page

Length Guidelines

  • Word Count: Minimum 70,000 words
  • Page Count: Approximately 280+ pages
  • Chapter Length: 2,000-4,000 words per chapter
  • Series Potential: Standalone or series welcome

Required Documents

  1. 1
    Query Letter (1 page maximum)

    Professional introduction with book summary and author credentials

  2. 2
    Synopsis (2-3 pages maximum)

    Complete plot summary including the ending

  3. 3
    Sample Chapters (First 3 chapters or 50 pages)

    Opening chapters that demonstrate your writing style

  4. 4
    Author Biography (1 page maximum)

    Publishing history, relevant experience, and platform

Complete Manuscript

Your novel must be complete before submission. We do not accept proposals for unfinished works.

Exclusive Submissions

Please submit exclusively to Rhubarb Press during our 12-week review period. Multiple submissions will be rejected.

No AI Content

We do not accept manuscripts created with or assisted by artificial intelligence tools.

Query Letter Guidelines

Your query letter is your first impression. Follow industry standards used by major publishers like HarperCollins and Penguin Random House:

Opening Paragraph

  • • Book title and word count
  • • Genre and word count
  • • Brief hook/logline
  • • Reason for choosing Rhubarb Press

Middle Paragraph(s)

  • • Plot summary (2-3 paragraphs)
  • • Main character introduction
  • • Central conflict/mystery
  • • Comparable titles

Closing Paragraph

  • • Author credentials
  • • Relevant experience
  • • Platform/following
  • • Professional closing

Synopsis Guidelines

Essential Elements

  • Complete plot summary from beginning to end
  • Major characters and their motivations
  • Key plot points and turning moments
  • Resolution and reveal of the mystery
  • Third-person present tense

Industry Standards

Following practices established by major publishers, your synopsis should reveal the entire plot, including the murderer's identity and resolution method. Do not withhold the ending to create suspense.

Length Requirements

2-3 pages maximum, single-spaced, approximately 500-750 words

Submission Process

How to Submit

1

Email all materials to: submissions@rhubarbpress.com

2

Subject line: "SUBMISSION: [Your Book Title] by [Your Name]"

3

Attach all documents as separate files (do not embed in email body)

4

Include a brief cover email introducing your submission

Response Timeline

Acknowledgment

Within 5 business days of submission

Initial Review

4-6 weeks for query and sample chapters

Full Manuscript Request

8-12 weeks for complete manuscript review

Final Decision

All authors receive a response, positive or negative

Personal Attention

Every submission receives careful consideration from our editorial team.

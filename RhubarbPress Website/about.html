About Rhubarb Press

Our story, our mission, our commitment to independent publishing

Our Story

"Every great story deserves to be heard."

Rhubarb Press was incorporated in August 2025 as a small family-run publishing house with a singular mission: to give hitherto unknown authors a voice in an increasingly crowded literary landscape. Born from a deep love of storytelling and a belief that exceptional narratives can emerge from the most unexpected places, we set out to champion writers whose voices might otherwise go unheard.

Our first and main imprint focuses on Cosy Crime, a genre particularly close to our hearts. There's something uniquely British about the cosy mystery tradition—those intimate, character-driven stories where the puzzle matters as much as the people solving it. We believe this genre offers the perfect canvas for new voices to explore human nature, community dynamics, and the timeless appeal of justice served with a cup of tea.

Formed in London with deep roots stretching to Peckham, Cleethorpes, and Dundee, Rhubarb Press embodies a uniquely diverse British perspective. These connections across England and Scotland inform our broad global outlook—we understand that great stories transcend geographical boundaries while remaining deeply rooted in the places that shape them.

Our Mission

At Rhubarb Press, we believe that publishing should be a collaborative journey between author and publisher. We work closely with our writers from manuscript to market, providing the support, guidance, and creative partnership that helps transform promising drafts into compelling published works.

Our commitment extends beyond simply printing books. We're dedicated to:

  • Nurturing emerging talent in the cosy crime genre
  • Maintaining the highest editorial and production standards
  • Building genuine relationships with our authors and readers
  • Supporting diverse voices and perspectives in publishing
  • Celebrating the craft of storytelling in all its forms

What We Stand For

Passion for Stories

Every manuscript we consider receives our full attention and genuine enthusiasm for the story within.

Community First

We're building a community of writers and readers who share our love for well-crafted mysteries.

Quality Excellence

From editing to design, we maintain the highest standards in every aspect of book production.

Global Perspective

While rooted in British tradition, we embrace stories that speak to readers worldwide.

Looking Forward

As we grow, our commitment remains unchanged: to discover, develop, and champion the next generation of cosy crime writers. Whether you're a seasoned author looking for a new home or a debut novelist with a story to tell, we'd love to hear from you.
